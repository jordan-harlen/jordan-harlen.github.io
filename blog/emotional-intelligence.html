What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is a persons ability to identify and manage their emotions and to understand and respond to the emotions of others.

How is it different to IQ?

EQ is a measurement of your ability to indentify, manage emotions and your awareness of how these can be used to make decisions.

IQ is a measurement of you ability to problem solve and understand or articulate complex ideas.

Why is it important?

Having a high EQ is important for many reasons.

People with a high EQ work better in team enviroments and hold strong relationships. They manage change in life better and have a higher capability to deal with stress. They are generally happier and have a positive wellbeing than those with a lower EQ. A high EQ also mean less stress through the ability to deal with it better and a higher motivation to achieve things.
